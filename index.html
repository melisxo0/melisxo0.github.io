Traveling is the most beautiful way to gather new inspiration. Europe has so many fascinating corners to offer, but three city trips have stayed particularly memorable for me. On this page, I take you along to my three personal favorites.
Each of these destinations has a completely unique character. From Mediterranean romance to impressive history and architectural masterpieces.

Ready for the journey?
Here they are:

Valencia - Spain

Bienvenidos a Valencia! Valencia absolutely enchanted me. The wonderfully warm weather and the heartfelt openness of the people made me feel welcome immediately. There is a very special energy there that instantly infects you and invites you to explore.
Culinary-wise, the trip was also a real highlight; the food was simply delicious. Whether a traditional paella directly by the sea or small tapas in the old town, every bite was a delight. I was particularly impressed by the incredible diversity of the city: whether during the day in the futuristic parks or at night in the lively neighborhoods, there was always something to experience and discover.
Valencia is the perfect mix of enjoyment and energy, which I can only recommend to everyone. The combination of cutting-edge architecture, historic squares, and the relaxed beach vibe makes this city one of my absolute favorite places in Europe.

Venice - Italy

Ciao, Venezia! After graduation, my friends and I went straight to Venice, and it could not have been more beautiful. The city immediately captivated us with its romantic atmosphere and provided the perfect setting to celebrate the end of our school years.
It was wonderfully warm, and the city is simply extraordinary because of all the water. It is fascinating to see how the glittering sunlight on the canals gives off its own kind of magic. Wandering through the narrow, winding streets without cars and doing everything by boat was a real experience that you can't find anywhere else. In this special setting, you completely forget the time.
Our trip was crowned by the fantastic food we enjoyed on mild summer evenings in the small trattorias right on the water. From classic pasta to the typical Venetian specialties, every moment was simply pure enjoyment.

London - England

London is calling! London absolutely enchanted me from the very first second! Even though it was quite cold during my visit, the city more than made up for it with its incredible history and special energy.
The architecture is simply beautiful and fascinating: at every corner, you encounter the contrast between venerable monuments like Big Ben and the hyper-modern skyline of the City. It is so much fun to simply stroll through the different neighborhoods and take in the imposing facades.
A particular highlight was my visit to Chinatown. The bright colors, the hanging lanterns, and the lively atmosphere there simply have to be experienced; it feels like a little world trip right in the middle of the city. Despite the chilly temperatures, it was an all-around perfect trip that definitely makes you want more!
